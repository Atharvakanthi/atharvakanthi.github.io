Blog About

Hello, I'm Atharva!

I'm an economics graduate passionate about applying data and theory to understand complex policy challenges. I hold an MSc in Economics from the University of Nottingham and a BA in Economics from Wilson College, Mumbai University.

Through independent projects and academic research, I’ve explored topics such as exchange rate regimes, growth volatility, inflation dynamics, and macroeconomic forecasting using tools like STATA, Python, MATLAB, and Excel.

I also bring practical experience through roles in operations, sales, and student leadership. My work has taken me from managing large events at Kalangan to coordinating youth parliaments and academic discussions.

This blog is my way of bridging theory with practice — whether it’s analyzing India’s inflation puzzle or comparing South Asian economies through a VAR model. I believe in writing that’s not just accurate, but accessible.
